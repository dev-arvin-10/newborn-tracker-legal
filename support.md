# Newborn Tracker Support

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I start tracking?
Tap any tracker marble on the home screen to log an activity.

### How do I manage my subscription?
Go to iOS Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → Newborn Tracker

### How do I cancel my subscription?
Go to iOS Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → Newborn Tracker → Cancel Subscription

### Where is my data stored?
All data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to external servers.

### How do I backup my data?
Data is included in your iOS device backup (iCloud or iTunes).

### How do I restore my subscription on a new device?
Open the app → Settings → Restore Purchases

### What happens during the free trial?
You get full access to all features for 30 days. No payment is required during the trial period.

### When will I be charged?
You will be charged $0.99 after your 30-day free trial ends, unless you cancel before then.

### How do I get a refund?
Refunds are handled by Apple. Go to reportaproblem.apple.com or contact Apple Support directly.

### The app isn't working properly. What should I do?
Try restarting the app first. If the problem persists, contact us using the information below.
