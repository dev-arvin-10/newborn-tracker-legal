Privacy Policy for Newborn Tracker

Last updated: December 14, 2024

Data Collection

Newborn Tracker does NOT collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All data is stored locally on your device.

Data Stored Locally

The app stores the following data on your device:

  • Baby profile information (name, date of birth, photo)
  • Tracker entries (feeding, diaper, sleep records)
  • App settings and preferences
  • Trial and subscription status

Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties. Your data never leaves your device.

Subscription Information

Subscription purchases are processed by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information. Apple's privacy policy applies to subscription transactions.

Device Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Notifications: To send reminders for baby care activities
  • Photos: To add baby photos to profiles (optional)

Data Deletion

You can delete all app data by:

  1. Deleting the app from your device
  2. Using the "Clear All Data" option in app settings

Children's Privacy

This app is designed for parents and caregivers. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact
